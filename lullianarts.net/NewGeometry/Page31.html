1.3.20 Fig 30 - the 12 signs
FIGURE OF THE 12 SIGNS


This figure of the 12 signs of heaven is included in this science to enable one to imagine the distances of heaven and the planets, and also to imagine constellations. But we will speak briefly about this science here because we already dealt with it at length in the New Astronomy we wrote.
Now, a. stands for the complexion of fire, b. for earth, c. for air and d. for water. Aries is of the a. complexion, Taurus belongs to b., Gemini to c., Cancer to d., Leo to a., Virgo to b., Libra to c., Scorpio to d., Sagittarius to a., Capricorn to b., Aquarius to c. and Pisces to d.
Hence, when Saturn is in the house of Aries, the constellation is hot and dry, dry and cold, and because of the two instances of dryness in this constellation, earth is its ruler, which is unfortunate on account of hot and cold qualities, and as it is made of contraries, it is confused and cannot last very long. And if hot and dry Mars enters into this constellation, then the constellation has three instances of dryness, two of heat and one of cold, which is fortunate for earth and unfortunate for water, and this misfortune must last a long time. And if moist and warm Gemini enters into this constellation, then the constellation has three instances of dryness, but the one instance of cold and one of moisture are afflicted, whereas heat and dryness are fortunate, and earth most of all, because fire is dry on account of earth, so that moisture in this constellation is more afflicted than cold, because dryness in this constellation is more fortunate than fire. Following the example we gave with the said signs and planets, you can understand other constellations.
In the master figure, it is said that the circle is halfway between the major and minor squares, which means that there are 4 units between 8 and 12, and 4 units between 12 and 16. And given the orderly concordance between the quintessence and the 4 general substances of the world, namely the 4 elements, there must be order in heaven as there is order in the combined descent of the circle, triangle and square, so that there be an equal space of 4 equal units between the Moon and the outer surface of the sphere of fire, and from Saturn to heaven, with the planets in the middle between heaven and the sphere of fire, and as this is true, as required by the natural order between a cause and its effect, it is good to know this figure of the 12 signs and to visualize it following the process we described.
