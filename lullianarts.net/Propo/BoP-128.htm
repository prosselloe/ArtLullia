3 - Figure S.

The Purpose of Figure S.

S., as a creature, is a mirror reflecting likenesses of A.'s dignities since the goodness of A. created the goodness of S., and the greatness of A. magnified S. and made S. great in substance and virtue, and eternity created S. to be immortal, and the power of A. gave S. the power to remember, understand and love, and likewise with the other dignities of A. Now S. is included in this Art so that through the influence that S. receives from A., S. can investigate the likenesses of A., and find the particular truth it seeks, with the help of the propositions of S. devised for the said purpose.
