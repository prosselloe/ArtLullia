6 - Fig. Theology.

The Purpose of the Figure of Theology

The figure of Theology is included in this Art because Theology is a light and a way for S. to reach toward A. and speak about A., and through it S. receives blessings from A., and influences itself to receive blue V. against red V.; the propositions on Theology in this book were intended for this as well as for investigating particular issues so as to form a universal concept in accordance with the propositions.
