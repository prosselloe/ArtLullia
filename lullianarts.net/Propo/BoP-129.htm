The Purpose of Figure V.

V. is included in this Art for investigating the particular issues that S. considers and judges with respect to A. given that blue V. consists of the very likenesses that S. attracts from the ones that A. transmits. The Sun shines on fire, and fire as it absorbs sunlight in its brightness influences its own light and heat, and S. likewise produces blue V. by using likenesses transmitted to S. by A. But if S. neglects to use these likenesses or even turns against them, it produces red V. Because A. should be loved and understood by S. through blue V. and not hated or ignored through red V., S. can either be saved, or if it turns against A.. S. can be damned.  In view of what we said about S., the statements in V. are intended to guide the investigation of particular issues within their universal context.
