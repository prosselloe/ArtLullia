The Purpose of The Common Figure

A full compendium of all sciences is a most useful thing. Therefore the Common Figure was devised and included in this Art to serve as a full compendium of the other figures of this Art by reducing  the other figures of this Art to it. By turning its circles any particular can be found within its universality as cameras are matched with one another. This book was written to fit the size of this figure, which makes the Art portable.
