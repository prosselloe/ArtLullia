Bl. Raymond wrote the Book of Propositions about 1283 - 7 AD. This translation was made by Yanis Dambergs PhD from the Latin version of  Liber propositionum secundum Artem demonstrativam compilatus in Tome III, (503-563) of Raymundi Lulli Opera Omnia, Ivo Salzinger editor, 8 vols., Mainz, 1721-1742 (reprint Stegmüller Frankfurt, 1965). 

Here is the reference to the original as given in the Ramon Llull Database by Professor Anthony Bonner, on line at the University of Barcelona web site:

http://orbita.bib.ub.es/ramon/index.asp 

where you can find more information about the manuscripts, editions, catalogs, bibliographies etc. of:

Liber propositionum secundum Artem demonstrativam compilatus
Date: 1283-7 (?) Place:
Catalogue number: II.B.6
Short title: PropAD
MOG III, viii (503-563)

"MOG" which is short for Moguntia, or the Latin name for Mainz, is the logo used to refer to Ivo Salzinger's abovementioned 8 volume publication.
